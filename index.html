Our Programs

Undergraduate

An undergraduate degree in physics at MIT prepares students very well for graduate studies in physics, as well as for a variety of academic or research-related careers.

Graduate

Consistently highly ranked by U.S. News and World Reports as the Best Physics Program in the World.

Pappalardo Fellowships in Physics

Our distinguished postdoctoral fellowship program that identifies, recruits and supports talented and promising young physicists at an early stage of their careers.

Our Faculty

We have five faculty who have won the Nobel Prize in Physics. We have also been the source of innovation in physics education with eight Oersted Medal winners.
